Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Developer: Jeyhun Mammadov (Alpha Pulse)

Effective Date: May 10, 2026

Welcome to Quoom. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

  1. Data Collection: We do not collect any personal data. No registration, no email, no phone number required.
  2. Information Security: All communications are end-to-end encrypted using industry-standard protocols (NIP-44/NIP-17).
  3. Third-party Services: The app connects to third-party Nostr relays. These relays may see your IP address and encrypted metadata.
  4. Local Storage: All chat history and private keys are stored exclusively on your device in an encrypted format.
  5. Your Rights: As you own your keys, you have full control over your data and identity.
